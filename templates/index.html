Exploring Asheville's Airbnb Landscape

Airbnb

Airbnb is the world's biggest accomodation sharing-site with listings across 81,000 cities and 191 countries in the world. It provides more than 5 million spaces to stay of which over 660,000 listings are in the USA. With its ever growing number of online rental listings, it has successfully disrupted the hospitality industry as more and more travellers ditch high-priced hotels and resorts in favor of premier, cost-effective accomodations provided by Airbnb.

Asheville, North Carolina

Located in the Blue Ridge Mountains, Asheville is the largest city in western North Carolina and is known for its breathtaking ladscapes, vibrant art-scene and historic architecture. As one of the most sought after vacation spots for mountain lovers, the city offers a variety of stay options to travellers including about 2400 Airbnb rentals. Airbnb rental options in the city are a mixed bag of houses, apartments, secluded cabins, RV's and campers.

Our Focus - Exploratory Analysis with Web Visualizations

Through this project, our team of mountain loving Data Science Enthusiasts explore Airbnb's rental landscape in Asheville, North Carolina through various static and interactive web visualizations. This site provides information about the data set, analysis tools & processes employed and the results of this exploratory analysis.

Due to time limitations, the focus of this exploratory analysis is limited to the following:

  1. Identify the different types of properties for rent in Asheville
  2. Explore which Airbnb listings are rated highly by guests
  3. Explore how Airbnb rental prices vary by neighbourhood
