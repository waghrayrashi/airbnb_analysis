Description of Data

The dataset comprises of three main tables:

  • Listings: Detailed listings data showing 106 attributes for each listing
  • Reviews: Detailed reviews given by the guests (Date, listing_id, reviewer_id, comment)
  • Calendar: Provdies details of booking for teh next year by listing (listing_id, date, available, price)
