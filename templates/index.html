Exploring Asheville's Airbnb Landscape


Airbnb

Airbnb is the world's biggest accomodation sharing-site with listings across 81,000 cities and 191 countries in the world. It provides more than 5 million spaces to stay of which over 660,000 listings are in the USA. With its ever growing number of online rental listings, Airbnb has successfully disrupted the hospitality industry as more and more travellers ditch high-priced hotels and resorts in favor of premier, cost-effective accomodations provided by Airbnb.

Asheville, North Carolina

Located in the Blue Ridge Mountains, Asheville is the largest city in western North Carolina and is known for its breathtaking ladscapes, vibrant art-scene and historic architecture. As a vacation hotspot, the city offers a variety of stay options to travellers including Airbnb rentals. Airbnb options in this area are a mixed bag of houses, apartments, secluded cabins and RV's and campers.

Our Focus

Through this project, our team of mountain loving Data Enthusiasts attempt to explore Airbnb's rental landscape in Asheville, North Carolina. This site provides information of this exploratory analysis, including the dataset, analysis tools & processes employed and the results of teh analysis using static and interactive visualizations.

Due to time limitations, the Data Enthusiasts decided to focus our exploratory analysis on the following:

  1. What are the different types of properties for rent in Asheville? How do they vary by neighbourhood?
  2. Which listings/localities are rated highly by guests?
  3. How do rental prices vary by location?
